About this app

The purpose of this app is to assist the intermediate Quran reading student. There are learning opportunities for all levels of proficiency, from just starting to just needing practice.

Tajweed Rules Covered

  • Qalqalah
  • Regular Madd
  • Ghunnah
  • Idghaam with no ghunnah
  • Idghaam with ghunnah
  • Ikhfa
  • Iqlaab
  • Hamzatul Wasl
  • Elongated Madd
